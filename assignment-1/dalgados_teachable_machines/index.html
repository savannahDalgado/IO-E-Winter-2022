Project 1

Project 1 - Formstorming

When starting this project, I was a little confused on where I should start and what kind of iterations I should go with. When exploring with teachable machines and p5, I learned how to impletment gifs into p5 successfully, and I learned just how sensitive the object recognition is on the Teachable Machines website. I tried to go past my comfort zone, and a couple times it didn't totally work out but I'm still glad that I tried nonetheless. For one of my objects my plan was to use my tamagotchi, but I ended up using photos I found online instead and showed them to the camera from my phone as it was easier for the AI to read what images were displayed as opposed to the tiny tamagotchi screen. The rest of my objects were figurines that I have collected over the years from TV shows and movies that I enjoy.
